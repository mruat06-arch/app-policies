Privacy Policy

ZoneSync — World Clock & Timezone Converter

Effective Date: February 26, 2026

Murat Can Cicek ("we", "our", or "us") built ZoneSync to help you manage multiple time zones, convert times, and plan meetings across different regions. Your privacy is fundamentally important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information ZoneSync collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

ZoneSync collects only the following minimal data:

  • Your favorite cities and timezone preferences, stored locally on your device
  • Anonymous crash reports to improve app stability
  • Aggregated usage statistics (e.g., feature usage counts) to improve the app experience

These analytics contain no personally identifiable information and cannot be linked back to you.

Local Data Storage

All of your timezone preferences, favorite cities, and meeting plans are stored locally on your device. No user accounts or authentication are required to use ZoneSync.

Widgets

ZoneSync offers home screen and lock screen widgets that display time zone information. Widgets only access timezone and clock data, which is not personal information. No personal data is collected or transmitted through widget functionality.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, rent, or trade your personal data with any third parties. Your preferences and data stay on your device.

Advertising

ZoneSync does not display advertisements and does not use any ad tracking technologies.

Data Deletion

You can delete all of your data at any time from within the app or by removing the app from your device. Once deleted, your data is permanently removed.

Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features, including encryption and device passcode.

Children's Privacy

ZoneSync is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

GDPR Compliance

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your data at all times. You may exercise your rights by managing or deleting data directly within the app.

CCPA Compliance

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information is collected and to request its deletion. ZoneSync does not sell personal information. You can delete all your data by removing the app from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on this page with a revised effective date.
