Frequently Asked Questions

How do I humanize my text?

Simply paste or type your AI-generated text into the input area, then tap the "Humanize" button. The app will instantly transform your text into a more natural, human-sounding version.

Can I adjust the tone and style?

Yes! Humanize offers adjustable tone and style settings. You can choose from different writing styles such as casual, professional, academic, or creative to match your needs.

Is my text stored or sent to any server?

No. All text processing is performed entirely on your device. Your text is never uploaded to our servers or shared with any third party. Your privacy is our top priority.

What types of text can I humanize?

You can humanize any AI-generated text, including essays, emails, articles, social media posts, and more. The app works best with English text.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. To cancel, go to your device's Settings > tap your name > Subscriptions > select Humanize > Cancel Subscription. Your premium features will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

Is there a limit to how much text I can process?

Free users have a daily limit on text transformations. Premium subscribers enjoy unlimited text processing with no restrictions.

The app is not working properly. What should I do?

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, make sure you have the latest version installed from the App Store. You can also contact us at the email above for further assistance.
