Privacy Policy

RoomAI — AI-Powered Room Transformation

Effective Date: March 5, 2026

Murat Can Cicek ("we", "our", or "us") built RoomAI to help you visualize room redesigns using artificial intelligence. We take your privacy seriously, especially regarding your photos and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use RoomAI.

1. Photos & Camera Access

RoomAI requires access to your device's camera and photo library to capture or select room photos for AI transformation. Important details:

  • Photos you select or capture are sent to Google's Gemini AI service solely for the purpose of generating room transformations.
  • Photos are transmitted securely using HTTPS encryption.
  • We do not store your original or transformed photos on any server. All images are processed in real time and returned to your device.
  • Transformed results are stored locally on your device only using on-device storage (SwiftData).
  • You can delete your transformation history at any time from within the app.
  • You can revoke camera and photo library access at any time in Settings > Privacy > Camera / Photos.

2. AI Processing

RoomAI uses Google's Gemini AI to generate room transformations. When you initiate a transformation:

  • Your room photo and selected style preferences are sent to Google's Gemini API.
  • Google processes the image to generate the transformed result.
  • Google's use of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy and their Gemini API Terms of Service.
  • We do not use your photos to train AI models. Google's Gemini API for paid API usage does not use submitted data for model training.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Transformation history: Original and transformed images, room type, style, and date — stored via SwiftData.
  • Usage data: Daily transformation count for free tier limits — stored in UserDefaults.
  • Preferences: Onboarding status, appearance mode — stored in UserDefaults.

None of this data is transmitted to our servers or any third party (except as described in the AI Processing section above).

4. Subscription & Purchase Data

RoomAI offers optional premium subscriptions managed through Apple's App Store and RevenueCat:

  • All payment processing is handled by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment information (credit card numbers, billing address, etc.).
  • We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app user ID to track entitlement status. No personally identifiable information is shared with RevenueCat.
  • RevenueCat's privacy practices are described in their Privacy Policy.

5. No User Accounts

RoomAI does not require user accounts or authentication. No personal information such as name, email address, or phone number is collected by the app.

6. Analytics & Tracking

RoomAI does not include any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. We do not collect usage analytics, crash reports, or behavioral data. We do not track you across apps or websites.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, rent, or trade your personal data, photos, or any other information with third parties, except:

  • Google Gemini API: Photos are sent for AI processing as described above.
  • RevenueCat: Anonymous subscription status as described above.
  • Apple: Standard App Store purchase processing.

8. Children's Privacy

RoomAI is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

9. Data Retention

Since all user data is stored locally on your device:

  • You can delete transformation history at any time within the app.
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.
  • We have no server-side data to retain or delete.

10. Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • All network communication uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • API keys are securely embedded and not exposed to end users.
  • Local data is protected by iOS's built-in data protection and sandboxing.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
