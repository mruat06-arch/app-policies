Frequently Asked Questions

How does journaling work in SoulScribe?

Simply tap the "+" button to create a new journal entry. You can write freely, choose a category (reflection, prayer, gratitude, or general), add tags, and record your mood. Your entries are saved automatically and organized by date for easy browsing.

How do AI insights work?

When you have enough journal entries, SoulScribe's AI analyzes patterns in your writing to surface themes, emotional trends, and reflections you might not have noticed. AI insights are generated in real time and your journal content is not stored on any external server. You can enable or disable this feature at any time in Settings.

Is my journal data private?

Yes. All of your journal entries, prayers, and reflections are stored locally on your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, data is synced only across your own Apple devices. We never access, read, or share your personal content.

How do I manage my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to your device's Settings > tap your name > Subscriptions > select SoulScribe. From there you can change your plan or cancel. Your premium features remain active until the end of your current billing period.

Can I export my journal entries?

Yes. Go to Settings > Export Data to export your journal entries as a PDF or text file. This is a great way to keep a personal backup or print your reflections.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings > Delete All Data to permanently remove all journal entries, prayers, and reflections from your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, this will also remove data from your other synced devices.

Are AI insights the same as counseling or therapy?

No. AI insights are for personal reflection only and are not a substitute for professional counseling, therapy, or spiritual direction. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact a qualified professional immediately.

Can I use SoulScribe offline?

Yes. SoulScribe works fully offline for journaling, prayer tracking, and gratitude entries. AI insights may require an internet connection depending on your device, but all core features are available without connectivity.
