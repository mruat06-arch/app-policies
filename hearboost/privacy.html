Privacy Policy

HearBoost — Hearing Aid, Hearing Test & Sound Amplification

Effective Date: February 26, 2026

Murat Can Cicek ("we", "our", or "us") built HearBoost to help you hear better in everyday situations. We take your privacy seriously, especially regarding audio data and health information. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled.

Microphone Access

HearBoost requires access to your device's microphone to provide real-time sound amplification. Important details about microphone usage:

  • Audio is processed in real time on your device only.
  • Audio is never recorded, stored, or transmitted to any server.
  • Audio data exists only momentarily during live amplification and is immediately discarded.
  • You can revoke microphone access at any time in your device's Settings > Privacy > Microphone.

Hearing Test Data

HearBoost includes a hearing test feature. Your hearing test results are:

  • Stored locally on your device only.
  • Used to personalize your amplification profile.
  • Never sent to external servers or shared with third parties.

iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled, your hearing test results and amplification settings may be synced across your own Apple devices using your personal iCloud account. This sync is managed entirely by Apple's iCloud infrastructure and is accessible only to you. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, rent, or trade your personal data, hearing test results, or any audio data with any third parties.

No User Accounts

HearBoost does not require user accounts or authentication. No personal information such as name, email, or phone number is collected by the app.

Analytics

We collect anonymous crash reports and aggregated usage statistics to improve app stability and performance. These analytics contain no personally identifiable information, no audio data, and cannot be linked back to you.

Advertising

HearBoost does not display advertisements and does not use any ad tracking technologies.

Data Deletion

You can delete all of your data (hearing test results and settings) at any time from within the app. Once deleted, your data is permanently removed from your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, deletion will also propagate to your other synced devices.

Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features, including encryption and device passcode. iCloud data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple.

Children's Privacy

HearBoost is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

GDPR Compliance

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your data at all times. You may exercise your rights by managing or deleting data directly within the app.

CCPA Compliance

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information is collected and to request its deletion. HearBoost does not sell personal information. You can delete all your data from within the app at any time.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on this page with a revised effective date.
