Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new timezone?

Tap the "+" button on the main screen and search for a city or timezone. Select the one you want, and it will be added to your list of tracked time zones.

How do I remove a timezone?

Swipe left on any timezone in your list to see the delete option. You can also tap "Edit" to rearrange or remove multiple timezones at once.

How do I set up a widget?

Long-press on your home screen, tap the "+" button in the top corner, search for "ZoneSync", and choose your preferred widget size. You can configure which timezones appear in the widget by tapping on it after adding.

How does the meeting planner work?

Open the Meeting Planner from the main menu. Select the time zones of all participants, and ZoneSync will show you overlapping business hours and suggest the best meeting times for everyone.

How do I convert a specific time between zones?

Tap on any timezone in your list, then tap "Convert Time". Select the source and destination time zones, enter the time you want to convert, and the result will be displayed instantly.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. To cancel, go to your device's Settings > tap your name > Subscriptions > select ZoneSync > Cancel Subscription. Your premium features will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

Does ZoneSync automatically update for daylight saving time?

Yes! ZoneSync automatically adjusts for daylight saving time changes based on the latest timezone database. No manual updates are needed.

Is my data shared with anyone?

No. Your timezone preferences and data are stored locally on your device and are never shared with any third parties.
