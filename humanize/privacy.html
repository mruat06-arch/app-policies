Privacy Policy

Humanize — AI Text Humanizer

Effective Date: February 26, 2026

Murat Can Cicek ("we", "our", or "us") built Humanize to help you convert AI-generated text into natural, human-sounding text. Your privacy is fundamentally important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information Humanize collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

On-Device Processing

All text processing and humanization is performed locally on your device. Your text content is never uploaded to our servers or shared with any third party. This privacy-first approach ensures that your content remains entirely under your control.

Information We Collect

Humanize collects only the following minimal data:

  • Anonymous crash reports to improve app stability
  • Aggregated usage statistics (e.g., feature usage counts) to improve the app experience

These analytics contain no personally identifiable information and cannot be linked back to you or your text content.

Authentication

Humanize uses Firebase anonymous authentication to manage subscription status. No personal information such as your name, email, or phone number is collected during this process. Anonymous authentication generates a random identifier that is not tied to your identity.

iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled, your preferences and settings may be synced across your Apple devices using your personal iCloud account. This sync is managed entirely by Apple's iCloud infrastructure and is accessible only to you. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, rent, or trade your personal data or text content with any third parties. Your text stays on your device at all times.

Advertising

Humanize does not display advertisements and does not use any ad tracking technologies.

Data Deletion

You can delete all of your data at any time from within the app. Since text processing is performed on-device and not stored on our servers, deleting the app removes all associated data from your device.

Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features, including encryption and device passcode. iCloud data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple.

Children's Privacy

Humanize is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

GDPR Compliance

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Since all text processing is performed on-device and no personal data is stored on our servers, you have full control over your data at all times.

CCPA Compliance

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information is collected and to request its deletion. Humanize does not sell personal information. You can delete all your data by removing the app from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on this page with a revised effective date.
