Privacy Policy

Nooksy — AI Room Transformation

Effective Date: March 5, 2026

Murat Can Cicek ("we", "our", or "us") built Nooksy to help you visualize room redesigns using AI technology. Your privacy is fundamentally important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information Nooksy collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

Photos and Camera

Nooksy requires access to your device's camera or photo library to capture photos of your room for AI transformation. Your photos are processed as follows:

  • Your room photo is sent to our secure cloud service (Gemini AI) solely for the purpose of generating the room transformation.
  • Photos are processed in real-time and are not stored on our servers after the transformation is complete.
  • Transformed results are stored locally on your device only.
  • We do not use your photos for training AI models or any other purpose.

Information We Collect

Nooksy collects only the following minimal data:

  • Anonymous crash reports to improve app stability
  • Aggregated usage statistics (e.g., feature usage counts) to improve the app experience

These analytics contain no personally identifiable information and cannot be linked back to you or your photos.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, rent, or trade your personal data or photos with any third parties. Your photos are used exclusively for generating room transformations and are not retained after processing.

Third-Party Services

Nooksy uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini AI: For AI-powered room transformation generation. Photos are sent for processing only and are not stored.
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management. RevenueCat processes only anonymous transaction data.

Advertising

Nooksy does not display advertisements and does not use any ad tracking technologies.

Data Storage

All transformation history, preferences, and settings are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. You can delete all data at any time by deleting the app.

Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features, including encryption and device passcode. All network communications use HTTPS encryption.

Children's Privacy

Nooksy is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

GDPR Compliance

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Since photos are not stored on our servers and all history is local to your device, you have full control over your data at all times.

CCPA Compliance

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information is collected and to request its deletion. Nooksy does not sell personal information. You can delete all your data by removing the app from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on this page with a revised effective date.
