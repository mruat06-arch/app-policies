Frequently Asked Questions

How does HairAI work?

HairAI uses advanced AI (Gemini) to generate realistic hairstyle previews on your selfie. Simply take a photo, choose a hairstyle or color, and see the result instantly.

Why doesn't the result look realistic?

For best results, ensure your selfie has good lighting, a clear face, and a simple background. Front-facing photos with hair visible work best.

How do I manage my subscription?

Go to your device's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions to manage or cancel your HairAI subscription.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open HairAI > Settings > Restore Purchases. Your subscription will be restored if it's still active.

Is my photo data safe?

Yes. Photos are processed in real-time for transformation and are not stored on our servers. All history is saved locally on your device only.

How many free transforms do I get?

Free users get 3 hairstyle transformations per day. The limit resets at midnight. Upgrade to Premium for unlimited transforms.
